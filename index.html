Learn About Microplastics

What are Microplastics?

Microplastics are tiny plastic particles that pose a significant environmental threat. They can come from a variety of sources including the breakdown of larger plastic items and microbeads in personal care products.

Solutions

To combat microplastic pollution, consider reducing plastic use, supporting policies that limit plastic production, and participating in clean-up initiatives.
